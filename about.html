🎬 About Movie Mandala

Discover your cinema circle — one recommendation at a time

What is Movie Mandala?

Movie Mandala is a content-based movie recommendation system that suggests similar movies based on a user's preferred movie — using genre, cast, and keyword similarity through cosine similarity and TF-IDF techniques.

Why we built this

Streaming platforms offer thousands of choices, making it time-consuming and confusing to pick what to watch. Movie Mandala solves this by suggesting movies based on genuine content similarity rather than generic trending lists.
